What is good design?

In my opinion the main use of a website is to not only distribute information to an audience but also to be visually appealing so that more people chose to use your website over other of lesser quality. For instance a user-interface that is simple,straightforward, and easy-to-use will see more net-traffic than others that may be harder to use due to the simple interface being more user-friendly for beginners. Visual aids such as a nice color scheme or layot can also set the tone for a website depending on the colors or layout chosen. For instance, Nike choses to use more black and white minimalistic apporaches along with photos of their latest ad campaigns for their website and has all items on their site categorized in an easy to find way including a search bar to help. A wbesite like this (not necessarily Nike) will almost always see more traffic than a website that looks like Craigslist. Met with a map and a bunch of bright blue wording on a blank white screen, this website looks like it hasnt been updated since it was created which in turn makes the site look less user-friendly and harder to use.
